# Privacy Policy

Petly uses cloud services to support account recovery, sync, membership, payment status, and AI chat.

## Data We Process

Petly may process:

- Device identifier used for automatic account login.
- User number, nickname, and recovery secret.
- Pet installation and growth data.
- Membership status and payment order status.
- AI chat messages when the user uses AI chat.
- Basic service logs required for security, debugging, and service reliability.

## How Data Is Used

Data is used to:

- Keep pet data synced across cloud services.
- Restore an account with user number and recovery secret.
- Check membership entitlement.
- Generate and process payment orders.
- Provide AI chat responses.
- Maintain service reliability and investigate bugs.

## Recovery Secret

The recovery secret can restore account data. Keep it private and do not share it with others.

## Contact

For privacy or data requests, contact the Petly team through the official website.
